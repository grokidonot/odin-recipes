Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Better than the typical poppers.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed
  • 12 slices bacon

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Step 2: Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended. Fill each jalapeno half with the cheese mixture. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon. Arrange bacon-wrapped peppers on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, about 15 minutes.
